Privacy Policy

Space Outpost · Last updated June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Space Outpost ("the App", "we", "us") handles information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

The App does not require an account and does not collect or store personal information on our own servers. Your game progress and settings are saved only on your device and are not transmitted to us.

Third-party advertising

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob, a service provided by Google. To serve and measure ads, AdMob may collect and process certain information automatically, including:

  • Device advertising identifiers (such as the IDFA on iOS)
  • Device information (model, operating system, language, coarse region)
  • Usage and diagnostic data related to ad delivery and performance

This data is handled by Google under its own policies. See Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

Tracking and your choices (App Tracking Transparency)

On iOS, if personalized advertising is used, the App will ask for your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before any tracking identifier is used. You can decline, and you can change this at any time in Settings › Privacy & Security › Tracking. Declining does not stop ads from showing; it limits ad personalization.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will address it.

Data retention

Because we do not collect personal data, we have nothing to retain or delete on our side. Game data stored on your device is removed when you delete the App or reset progress in the App's settings. Data collected by Google AdMob is retained per Google's policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
